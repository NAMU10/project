We picked our side… Have you?
Join us in the movement for clean energy.

Going solar is about making a profitable and reliable investment, but it’s foremost about taking responsibility!

Our Vision

Our planet is our legacy, and there’s only one. The extensive use of fossil fuel energy has caused irreversible damage. The more we wait, the worse it will get. California’s Central Coast receives a practically unlimited amount of sunlight- why wouldn’t we harness it to do our part to protect the environment? Together, let’s choose a more responsible way to power our lives: Let’s go solar.

Our Mission

Solar has suffered and is still suffering from many misconceived notions, and we understand that the process of going solar might seem overwhelming. Education and empowerment are at the core of our approach.
Within our process, we meet with our clients to help them define their goals, evaluate their options, and make informed and confident decisions. We take care of the entire process, from design to engineering, permitting, installation and monitoring, so that going solar is a seamless and hassle-free process.


Now is the time.

The Central Coast of California is undoubtedly one of the most beautiful places on Earth, and we’re so grateful tolive here. We believe that it is everyone’s responsibility to protect our planet and our unique environment. Fossil fuels have helped our societies develop and industrialize, but we are now discovering their limits. We have already begun to pay the price: air and water pollution, wild habitat destruction, a changing climate, and political turmoil. The transition to a cleaner and more respectful energy system is critical to future generations.

Solar makes sense.

Solar technology has proven to not only be the most efficient energy alternative, but it is also an extremely profitable and reliable investment. Building owners who make the switch not only generate clean energy, but they will also make thousands of dollars in savings over the solar system’s lifespan. Home and commercial building owners are losing money every month on their electricity bills. Investing in solar gives one control of their energy costs, and once the system itself is paid for, electricity becomes free for the next 20 or so years!

Solutions.

The poor aesthetic of traditional solar systems shouldn’t deter you, as it is a problem of the past. This is how we do solar differently: by providing a full range of options, including aesthetically pleasing solutions for people concerned about the look of solar panels on top of their roof. We work with building integrated photovoltaics (BIPVs) to blend the system into the building’s structure to make it virtually seamless.
